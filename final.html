About Me

Hi there! I'm Justin Bengao, a passionate photographer and web developer currently studying at the University of the Cordilleras, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) with a specialization in web development.

My journey in technology and creativity goes hand in hand. While I'm diving deep into the world of web development, learning the latest technologies and frameworks, I also have a deep love for photography. I believe that both fields share a common thread - they're about capturing moments, creating experiences, and bringing visions to life.

When I'm not coding or behind the camera, you'll find me embracing the great outdoors. I'm an adventure enthusiast who loves exploring new places and capturing the beauty of nature through my lens. Most of my free time is spent playing outdoor sports and hiking through scenic trails, always with my camera ready to capture that perfect shot.

My approach to photography is instinctive and authentic - I don't follow rigid rules or specific styles. Instead, I let the moment guide me, capturing genuine emotions and natural beauty as I see them. This same philosophy carries over to my web development work, where I focus on creating intuitive, user-friendly experiences.

Whether it's developing a website or capturing a moment in time, I'm passionate about creating something meaningful that connects with people.
